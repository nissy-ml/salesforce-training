# Day 1 - CRM Basics (Salesforce Training)

## 1. What is CRM?
CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is a business strategy and software system used to manage all interactions between a company and its customers.

It helps companies understand their customers better, improve communication, and build long-term relationships.

---

## 2. Why do companies use Salesforce?
Companies use Salesforce because it helps them:

- Keep all customer information in one place
- Track leads and sales opportunities
- Automate daily business tasks
- Improve sales team productivity
- Provide better customer support

In simple terms, Salesforce helps businesses stay organized and grow faster.

---

## 3. Salesforce Key Objects

### 🏢 Account
Represents a company or organization a business deals with.

### 👤 Contact
Represents an individual person working in or related to an Account.

### 💰 Opportunity
Represents a potential sales deal or revenue-generating business chance.

---

## 4. Real-World Mapping (Easy Understanding)

Think like this:

- Account → A company like Infosys or TCS
- Contact → HR or employee inside that company
- Opportunity → A project or deal with that company

So Salesforce basically connects companies, people, and business deals.
