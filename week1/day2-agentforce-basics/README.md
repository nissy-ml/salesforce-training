# Day 2 - Agentforce 360 Basics

## 1. What is Agentforce 360?
Agentforce 360 is Salesforce’s AI-powered platform that helps businesses automate customer service, improve productivity, and enhance customer experiences through intelligent digital agents.

It combines CRM with AI to make business workflows smarter and faster.

---

## 2. Why do companies use Agentforce 360?
Companies use Agentforce 360 because it helps them:

- Provide quick customer support
- Automate repetitive business tasks
- Improve employee productivity
- Offer 24/7 assistance
- Enhance customer satisfaction

---

## 3. Core Features

### AI Automation
Automates repetitive support and business processes.

### Smart Customer Support
Uses AI agents to instantly respond to customer needs.

### Productivity Improvement
Helps teams focus on strategic tasks instead of routine work.

---

## 4. Real-World Mapping
